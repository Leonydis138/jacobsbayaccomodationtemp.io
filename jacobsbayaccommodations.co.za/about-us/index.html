Jacobs Bay Boutique Glamping & accommodation

A LITTLE PIECE OF OUTDOOR HEAVEN

The Plot Jacobs Bay, where stress and tension drains out of your body. The natural feel of stone and wood, this accommodation is under a natural tree canopy blocking the harsh African sun.

‘The Plot’ as we are affectionately known, includes the self-catering Jacobs Bay Boutique & Accommodation, The Pixie and Fairy Village, The West Coast Labyrinth and the Succulent Nursery. We are located just 400m from the beach. ‘The Plot’ is an ideal venue and accommodation for family get-togethers, weddings, weekend getaways and corporate events.

There are plenty of private areas for entertaining for groups and couples. Facilities include female and male ablutions and a communal kitchen. There is a play area for kids and our front office can arrange any local tours.

The shop sells ice and firewood and the Pixie Shop offers curios, fairies, pixies, dreamcatchers and kiddies’ toys. The Succulent Nursery sells a wide variety of cacti, succulents, native plants and rock art. The new West Coast Labyrinth is free for visitors to walk and enjoy.
