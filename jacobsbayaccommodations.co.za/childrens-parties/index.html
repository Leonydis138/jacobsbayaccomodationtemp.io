FUN IN THE PIXIE GARDEN

The Pixie and Fairy Village is a magical venue for children's birthday parties.

This delightful venue offers a unique opportunity to have an unrivalled birthday experience. Set up your party amongst the trees where tables and chairs are already provided, layout a lush picnic on the lawn, or throw a sausage sizzle on the fire. Braai and picnic areas are dotted around the lush gardens and are open for day visitors to use.

The highlight of the birthday festivities is to visit The Pixie and Fairy Village, where you may meet Mama Pixie, who will tell a story or two about the pixies in the village. Mama Pixie is the creator and visionary of this unique tourist attraction which has impressed adults and children alike.
