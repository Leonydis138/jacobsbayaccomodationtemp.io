Jacobsbaai Boutique Glamping & Accommodation

Accommodation at the Oasis includes double rooms, dorms, an A-Frame glamping tent with an ensuite, ensuite three-sleeper stone cottage, camping sites and A-Frame camping tents. We are committed to providing self-catering accommodation in Jacobsbaai at affordable prices. Established in the 1990s, Jacobsbaai is known today as the ‘Oyster of the West Coast’. It is famous for the West Coast Bioreserve, seafood, flora and fauna, the white stone houses and 5 tranquil bays, and The World Class Pixie and Fairy Village.
